About Me

I've been teaching fitness classes since 2004 in and around the Philadelphia Suburbs. I teach Zumba, Zumba Toning, Zumba Gold, Barre, Cardio Barre and Pilates. My hoobies include: Vegan cooking, dancing, taking barre classes, strength training, walking, reading and learning more about the things that I teach.

Licenses and Certifications

My licenses and certifications are in Zumba, Zumba Toning, Zumba Kids, Phase II Mat Pilates, Red Hot Barre, AFAA Primary Group Exercieses, American Safety and Health Institute CPR, First Aid and AED
